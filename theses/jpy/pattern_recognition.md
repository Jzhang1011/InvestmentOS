# JPY Pattern Recognition

Patterns support analysis; they do not predict outcomes.

## Higher for Longer

**Signals**

- Sticky U.S. inflation
- Strong labor market
- Delayed Fed easing
- Wide yield spread

**JPY impact:** Strongly supportive

## Carry Expansion

**Signals**

- Low volatility
- Stable liquidity
- Wide carry spread
- Strong risk appetite

**JPY impact:** Strongly supportive

## Carry Unwind

**Signals**

- Rising volatility
- Credit stress
- Funding pressure
- Forced deleveraging

**JPY impact:** Strongly negative

## BOJ Normalization

**Signals**

- Persistent wage growth
- Services inflation
- Repeated hikes
- Higher JGB yields

**JPY impact:** Negative, magnitude depends on pace

## Currency Intervention

**Signals**

- Escalating MOF language
- Confirmed market action
- Short-covering
- Rapid volatility

**JPY impact:** Usually temporary unless policy coordination changes

## Imported Inflation

**Signals**

- Rising energy and food prices
- Household pressure
- Political intervention pressure
- BOJ inflation concern

**JPY impact:** Mixed

## Global Recession

**Signals**

- Falling PMIs
- Rising unemployment
- Wider credit spreads
- Central-bank easing

**JPY impact:** Negative through safe-haven demand and carry unwind

## Pattern Interaction Rules

- Higher for longer + carry expansion → strongly supportive.
- BOJ normalization + carry unwind → potential thesis reassessment.
- Imported inflation + accelerating wages → faster BOJ risk.
- Intervention + unchanged yield spread → likely temporary effect.
- Multiple aligned patterns matter more than one isolated pattern.
